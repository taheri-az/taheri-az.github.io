About Me
======

I am a PhD researcher at Northeastern University working on high-level planning and decision-making for autonomous systems in unknown environments. My research bridges formal methods and robotic autonomy to enable provably correct behavior in complex, uncertain settings.

Research Interests
======

- Temporal logic-based task planning (LTL, scLTL)
- Autonomous robot navigation in unknown environments
- Decision-making under uncertainty
- Multi-agent systems and coordination
