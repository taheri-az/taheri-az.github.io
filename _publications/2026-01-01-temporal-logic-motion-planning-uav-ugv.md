This paper presents a motion planning framework for a heterogeneous robot team consisting of an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) and an unmanned ground vehicle (UGV) operating in semantically unknown environments. The framework enables the team to satisfy complex temporal logic task specifications while exploring and learning the semantics of the environment online.
